Tourist Places in Bandung

Bandung offers a wide range of tourist attractions, from historical sites to natural wonders. Whether you're interested in culture, nature, or adventure, Bandung has something for everyone. Here are some must-visit tourist spots in and around the city.

Braga Street

Braga Street is one of the most iconic streets in Bandung, known for its vibrant atmosphere, colonial-era architecture, and lively caf√©s. It's a great place for a leisurely walk, shopping, or enjoying street performances.

Tangkuban Perahu

Tangkuban Perahu is an active volcano located just outside Bandung. It is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the region, offering panoramic views, sulfuric craters, and beautiful hiking trails.

Personal Rating:

Ciwidey

Ciwidey is a region located south of Bandung, famous for its hot springs, tea plantations, and Kawah Putih (White Crater). It's a serene getaway for those who enjoy nature and outdoor activities.

Personal Rating:

Lembang

Lembang is a highland area located just outside Bandung, known for its cool climate and scenic landscapes. Popular attractions include the Floating Market, De Ranch, and the Farmhouse Lembang.

Personal Rating:

Kawah Putih

Kawah Putih, or White Crater, is a stunning crater lake with turquoise water surrounded by white sulfuric sands. It's a popular spot for photography and nature walks.

Personal Rating:

Taman Hutan Raya (Tahura)

Taman Hutan Raya (Tahura) is a conservation park located in the northern part of Bandung. It's a great place to explore nature trails, caves, and waterfalls, perfect for hiking enthusiasts.
